"Lonely Prism"

scult_prism_final2.png

This is the first piece I created from my imagination alone. I suppose I really liked working with the color pink when I first started modeling. It evolved several times, from being sat on tiles to having a white wall backdrop. This angle came to me on accident while messing around in my workspace; I then adapted it to the camera, thus the product you see here - extremely minimalist and simplistic.
